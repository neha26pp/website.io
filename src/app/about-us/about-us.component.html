We are the Software Development Organization World Campus!

This organization is a PSU affiliated club for students, alumni, staff, and members of the general public who are interested in developing their skills related to software engineering, programming, technology, computer hardware, and other related topics. The organization was founded with the purpose of the further education and networking of students in the field of software development, engineering computer science.
